The Dynamic Legacy of RCB

A futuristic journey into the milestones that shaped Royal Challengers Bangalore's place in cricket history.

2008 – The Beginning

RCB debuted in IPL with Rahul Dravid leading a team full of potential, setting the foundations of an icon.

2011 – GayleStorm Era

Chris Gayle, AB de Villiers, and Virat Kohli stormed into the final, thrilling fans globally with powerful batting performances.

2013 – AB de Villiers' Genius

AB de Villiers showed masterclass innings time after time, becoming a cornerstone for RCB’s aggressive style.

2016 – Virat Kohli's Record Season

Kohli’s phenomenal 973 runs season remains unbeaten in IPL history, inspiring the whole franchise’s spirit.

2018 – A Season of Near Glory

Despite remarkable performances, RCB narrowly missed playoffs, fueling the determination of the franchise for the future.
